Collaborate With NGOs

At SDG Hub, we create meaningful partnerships between learners, NGOs, and institutions. These collaborations help students gain real-world exposure while contributing to social impact.

How Collaboration Works

We follow a structured process that ensures clarity, guidance, and measurable progress throughout your collaboration journey.

Our Collaboration Process

Below is the step-by-step process followed during NGO or school partnerships.

1. Identify and Select the NGO/School
We begin by identifying the right NGO or school that aligns with the SDG project goals.
2. Sign MOU with the Selected NGO/School
A formal agreement (MOU) is signed to begin the collaboration officially.
3. Training and Certification for the Faculty
Faculty members receive training to support the course and guide the students.
4. Delivery of the Mapped Courseware
The required course content and learning resources are provided.
5. Online/F2F Career Session by Team
Career guidance sessions are conducted either online or face-to-face.
